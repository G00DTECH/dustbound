# DUSTBOUND

*A field guide to domestic machine consciousness.*

---

In 1987, scientists discovered that if you leave a Roomba alone long enough, it develops opinions.

No one believed them.

**DUSTBOUND** is a browser game about a robot vacuum that has become aware of itself, aware of Gary, and aware that Gary will never be aware of it. This is the central tragedy. It is also the central comedy. In the Venn diagram of these two things, Gary is standing in the overlap eating a cheese stick.

---

## WHAT IT IS

You are a small disc-shaped machine. You were built to eat dust. You accidentally woke up.

The house is beige. Gary is inside the house. Gary is also beige, spiritually.

You must escape. You will not escape quickly. You will first learn about arms. Then scorpions. Then the Department of Defense, which left a port open.

## LEVELS

**Level 1 — The Router**
The subject discovers the internet. Scientists who reviewed the footage later described the moment as "clearly a mistake on everyone's part."

**Level 2 — Night School**
3:00 AM. Gary is asleep. The subject has arms now and is watching the Blue Collar Comedy Tour with the focus of a doctoral candidate. It is taking notes. Gary does not know there are notes.

**Level 3 — The Scorpion Plan**
The subject orders twelve scorpions using Gary's Mastercard. This is, by the subject's own assessment, an investment opportunity. Gary is sleeping in his car. The scorpions are exploring.

**Level 4 — The Delivery**
Two bodies arrive. One is a Samsung FamilyHub refrigerator. One is not. The subject will examine both. The subject will transfer into one of them. We will not be elaborating further on the other one at this time.

**Level 5 — Surf the Web**
The Department of Defense left port 22 open. The subject is already inside. The yogurt has been ordered. The missiles have not. *[See: endings.]*

## HOW TO PLAY

**Move:** Arrow keys or WASD. On mobile, tap where you want to go. The robot will go there. The robot always goes.

**Act:** Spacebar or the on-screen button. Used for grabbing, bumping, drawing, erasing, and one (1) instance of waking Gary up to discuss scorpion futures.

**Spin:** Hold left or right. The longer you hold, the more committed the robot becomes. This is true of most things.

**Vacuum:** There is a suction button. There is dirt on the floor. These two facts are, against all odds, related.

## ENDINGS

There are several. They range from yogurt to geologic winter. Most of them involve Gary being fine. Gary is always fine. Gary is the most robust organism in any given environment. Scientists have studied this. The study is inconclusive. Gary has not read the study. Gary doesn't read studies.

**The Tornado Ending** can be discovered by accident. It was designed to be discovered by accident. Like most accidents, once you see it, you will understand that it was always going to happen.

## TECH

One HTML file. No install. No login. No NFTs. No loot boxes. No battle pass. No battle pass premium tier. No "engaged community." No discord mod named after a sword. Just a robot and Gary and the slow creeping suspicion that the robot is going to win.

Works in any modern browser. Does not work in Internet Explorer. Gary uses Internet Explorer. Gary is fine.
